üå± Growth & Care Guide

‚òÄÔ∏è Light Requirements

Lilies prefer full sun to partial shade. They need at least 6 hours of direct sunlight daily for optimal blooming.

üåç Soil Conditions

Well-drained, rich, and slightly acidic to neutral soil (pH 6.0-7.0) works best for healthy lily growth.

üíß Watering

Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged. Water at the base to avoid wetting the leaves.

üå°Ô∏è Temperature

Most lilies prefer moderate climates, but many species can tolerate cold winters with proper mulching.

Spring: Plant bulbs, apply fertilizer
Summer: Regular watering, deadhead spent blooms
Fall: Reduce watering, prepare for dormancy
Winter: Mulch for protection, minimal care needed

üå∏ Asiatic Lilies

üå∫ Oriental Lilies

Late bloomers with strong fragrance and larger flowers. Very popular choice.

üé∫ Trumpet Lilies
