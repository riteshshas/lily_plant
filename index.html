About Lily Plant

The lily is a flowering plant of the genus Lilium. Lilies are known for their large, prominent flowers and are native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. They are commonly grown for their beauty and fragrance and are often used in gardens and floral arrangements.

Lilies come in a variety of colors including white, pink, orange, and yellow. They symbolize purity, renewal, and transience. They thrive in well-drained soils and sunny locations.
